01

Who Are Entrepreneurs?

Learning Goal

Today, I will discover what entrepreneurship means, identify entrepreneurs around me, and understand how they help our community.

Quick Definitions
Entrepreneur:
A person who starts their own business to solve problems and earn money.
Business:
Selling goods or services to customers who need them.
Customer:
A person who buys what you are selling.
Discovery Steps
ENGAGE - Think & Discuss
Look around your community. Point out 3 people who own their own businesses (market seller, barber, seamstress, pure water seller, food vendor, mobile money agent).
EXPLORE - My Activity Choices
Option A: Draw 3 business owners you know and label what they sell.
Option B: Write a list of 3 business owners and explain how they help people in your community.
EXPLAIN - Learn Together

02

Types of Businesses

Learning Goal

Today, I will explore different types of businesses and discover which type interests me most.

Quick Definitions
Product-Based Business:
Selling things people can touch and use (bread, clothes, shoes).
Service-Based Business:
Helping people do things (haircuts, phone repairs, car washing).
EXPLORE - Values That Matter

Good entrepreneurs have these values:

  • Honesty ‚Äì Always tell the truth to customers
  • Hard Work ‚Äì Work even when it's difficult
  • Punctuality ‚Äì Deliver on time, always
  • Respect ‚Äì Treat customers and helpers well
  • Responsibility ‚Äì Keep your promises

Bad Business Practices to Avoid:

  • Cheating customers with wrong measurements
  • Selling expired or fake products
  • Being rude or disrespectful
  • Not keeping your word
Your reputation is your most valuable asset! Customers remember how you treat them.
